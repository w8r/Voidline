13 November 2013 - The website is online!

We have just uploaded our basic website that provides brief information about the project's goals and its roadmap.

This is the first step (or maybe second) we have taken towards a great purpose: true, accessible, advanced knowledge!

Please feel free to browse the few pages of this website to familiarize yourself with what Voidline aims to achieve, and how.

We have also created a Facebook page, where you can easily stay up to date with news, or you can simply subscribe to our newsletter.

Feedback is extremely helpful at this point, so if you have absolutely any comment about this project we would love to hear about it, either via Facebook or via e-mail.

The next step is to try to put our thoughts, on how we envision the system would work, into graphic form, such as illustrations and videos, so that everyone would get a clear picture of what the system aims to be, and what to be expected of it. For that, though, we need your help to be able to pay some graphic artists to help us put our thoughts into images. So if you want to help this project, please kindly donate anything you can and are willing to, on this page.

Thank you very much, and you'll hear from us soon!
